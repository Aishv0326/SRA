Our Story

Our company began as Shree Rajanand Engineers Pvt. Ltd.—a name that reflects our strong foundation in construction and engineering. When we stepped into interiors, we wanted something simple, modern, and easy to connect with. That’s how SRE Interiors was born.

Under this name, we bring together construction, architecture, and interior design—all under one roof. We don’t just create spaces; we shape complete environments where structure, design, and functionality come together seamlessly.
