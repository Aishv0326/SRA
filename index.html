Our Story

Our company began as Shree Rajanand Engineers Pvt. Ltd. — a name that reflects our strong foundation in construction and engineering. But when it came to stepping into the world of interiors, we wanted something that felt simple, modern, and easy to connect with.

That's how SRE Interiors was born. Under this name, we bring together construction, architecture, and interior design—all under one roof. SRE Interiors is not just about creating spaces, but about shaping complete environments where structure, design, and functionality come together seamlessly.

With years of experience in the industry, we've built a reputation for excellence, quality, and attention to detail. Our team of skilled professionals is dedicated to turning your vision into reality, whether it's a residential project, commercial space, or industrial facility.
